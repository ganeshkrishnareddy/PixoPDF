Frequently Asked Questions

Will my high quality layouts look pixelated?

To prevent pixelation, choose the "High" resolution scale option which renders images at double density.

Why does it download as a ZIP?

If your PDF contains multiple pages, we package all rendered files into a single ZIP file locally so that you don't have to confirm multiple download dialogs.
