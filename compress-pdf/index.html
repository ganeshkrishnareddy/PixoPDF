Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if my PDF cannot be compressed further?

Some PDF files are already highly optimized. If our engine cannot reduce the file size further without degrading visual output, it will notify you honestly and let you download the original file instead.

Are files kept on your server?

No. All optimizations are carried out locally inside your browser sandbox memory. Your files never leave your device.
