Frequently Asked Questions

What type of signature does PixoPDF add?

PixoPDF adds a visual electronic signature overlay to your document. It does not perform cryptographic qualified certificate signing or digital key verification.

Are my signatures secure?

Yes. Your document buffers, text entries, and drawn signature image bytes are processed strictly in your local browser sandbox and never leave your device.
