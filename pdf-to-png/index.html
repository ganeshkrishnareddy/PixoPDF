Frequently Asked Questions

Are my files uploaded?

No. PixoPDF operates 100% locally. None of your document text content, canvas renders, or images are transmitted over the network.

How does PixoPDF package multiple pages?

If your PDF document contains more than one page, PixoPDF packages the generated PNG image collection into a single `.zip` file for quick download.
