Private Browser-Side PDF Watermarking

Unlike online tools that process file contents by uploading them onto cloud platforms, PixoPDF implements every step locally. No watermark text, filenames, or PDF files are transmitted over the network, guaranteeing offline data confidentiality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does PixoPDF add its own branding to my PDF?

No. PixoPDF operates as a genuinely free suite. We never embed watermarks, stamps, or promotional text headers into your output documents.

Can I rotate the watermark diagonal?

Yes. You can configure rotation presets (0°, 45°, -45°, or 90°) to position text diagonally across your document pages.
