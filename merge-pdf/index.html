Secure and Private PDF Merger

Unlike online tools that copy your PDF files onto cloud storage systems, PixoPDF handles all combining steps locally in your browser. Since none of your document metadata, structural elements, or signatures are sent over the network, your documents are not uploaded to a PixoPDF processing server.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a file count limit for merging?

PixoPDF supports merging up to 100 PDF documents at once, with an aggregate size threshold limit of 500 MB to prevent web browser tab execution delays.

Will the quality of my PDFs degrade after merging?

No. Our client-side merger copies raw page data blocks directly into the newly constructed document, preserving resolution and format properties without compression degradation.
