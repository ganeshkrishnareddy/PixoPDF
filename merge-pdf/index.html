Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a file count limit for merging?

ZeroPDF supports merging up to 100 PDF documents at once, with an aggregate size threshold limit of 500 MB to prevent web browser tab execution delays.

Will the quality of my PDFs degrade after merging?

No. Our client-side merger copies raw page data blocks directly into the newly constructed document, preserving resolution and format properties without compression degradation.
