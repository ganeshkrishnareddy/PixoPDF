How to Remove Pages from PDF Online

01

Choose Document

Choose the target PDF file from your device that contains unwanted pages.

02

Select Pages to Delete

Click page thumbnails to highlight and select which pages to delete.

03

Remove Pages

Click Remove Selected Pages. PixoPDF splits and merges structural streams in browser memory.

04

Download

Save the resulting smaller PDF locally onto your device.

Secure Page Deletion in Browser

Unlike online editors that upload your document layout to a backend processing server, PixoPDF processes files locally inside your browser sandbox memory. None of your document metrics, signatures, or page contents are sent over the network.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I undo a page removal?

Yes. While selecting pages in the grid workspace, clicking a page thumbnail a second time deselects it from being deleted. If you've already generated the output file, just click "Remove More Pages" to reload the original file.

Can I remove all pages from a PDF?

No. PixoPDF enforces a validation check to ensure at least one page remains in the exported PDF file.
