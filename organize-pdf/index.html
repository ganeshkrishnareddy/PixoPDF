Frequently Asked Questions

Does PixoPDF work on mobile phone touch screens?

Yes. PixoPDF provides explicit 'Move Up' and 'Move Down' buttons on every thumbnail card, ensuring easy page ordering on smartphones and tablets.

How many pages can I organize at once?

PixoPDF processes documents containing up to 100 pages smoothly inside default browser tabs. Exceeding this page count can trigger minor canvas rendering delay limits.
